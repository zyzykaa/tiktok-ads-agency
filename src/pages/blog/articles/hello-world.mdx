# The TikTok Algorithm Shift (2026 Update)

TikTok is moving faster than any paid channel in the US. What used to work for broad targeting and polished ads is losing steam. The algorithm now rewards speed, iteration, and creator-native storytelling.

## What Changed

The shift is simple: TikTok is prioritizing interest clusters over demographics. That means your creative matters more than your targeting.

- **Problem:** Broad campaigns with generic creatives are fading.
- **Solution:** Build a content funnel designed for hooks, retention, and repeat winners.

## The 3-Second Rule

If you do not win the first three seconds, nothing else matters. Lead with the most surprising claim, proof, or payoff.
