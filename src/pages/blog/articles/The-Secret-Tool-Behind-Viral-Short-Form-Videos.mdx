# 1. What Is Transitional Hooks?

Transitional Hooks is a platform that provides **ready-made video transition clips** designed to improve engagement in short-form content.

These clips act as **visual or audio connectors** between scenes, helping videos feel more fluid and engaging.

Instead of creating transitions manually, users can:
- Browse a library of viral hooks  
- Download them instantly  
- Plug them into their videos

# 2. What Problem Does It Solve?

Short-form content has one brutal reality:

👉 You have 1–3 seconds to stop the scroll.

Most creators fail because:

Transitional hooks solve this by:

Without transitions, videos feel “choppy.”
